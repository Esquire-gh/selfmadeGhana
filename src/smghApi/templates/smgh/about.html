About Us

Our Mission

SelfMadeGhana is a platform that is driven by its mission to give every ghanaian youth a chance at the life that they dream of. We believe that we can all be who we want to be if we understood a few principles and concepts. Our mission is to bring these concepts, principles and methodologies to every Ghanaian youth through short, precise and captivating articles that will challenge to be more than they are and to believe that they are the future.

What we stand for

Lifelong Learning, Excellence, Self empowerment, Habit-Focused Process, Creativity and Ingenuity. These are a few of the principles the we stand for. SelfMadeGhana is a symbol of these principles within the Ghanaian youth are we believe that if we get these across to every single person, we will be better off both as individuals and as a country.
